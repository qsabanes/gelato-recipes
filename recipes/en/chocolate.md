# Chocolate Gelato (800g)

Direct dark chocolate flavour, no chocolate bar, built solely from cocoa powder.

## Formulation

| Ingredient | Quantity (g) | Notes |
| :---- | :---- | :---- |
| **Whole milk** | **470g** | More milk to compensate for the liquid a bar would provide. |
| **Cream (35% fat)** | **90g** | Cocoa powder adds little fat, so we raise the cream. |
| **Pure cocoa powder** | **65g** | High dose. The star of the batch. |
| **Skimmed milk powder** | **30g** | MSNF to retain the air. |
| **Sucrose** | **60g** | Adjusted: cocoa powder is very bitter. |
| **Dextrose** | **58g** | Stable PAC; keeps the ice crystals under control. |
| **Inulin** | **15g** | Body and fine texture. |
| **Fine salt** | **1g** | Boosts the positive bitterness of the cocoa. |
| **Guar gum** | **0.5g** | 1/4 teaspoon. |

## Protocol

Mix all the powders (cocoa included) and add them to the milk and cream at **50 °C**. Run the immersion blender, raise to **85 °C** for 1 minute and leave to mature in the fridge for **12 hours**.

> The 100% powder version gives a cleaner flavour; a version with a (70%) bar would come out more pasty.
