# Hazelnut Gelato (800g)

## Formulation

| Ingredient | Quantity (g) | Notes |
| :---- | :---- | :---- |
| **Whole milk** | **470g** | Main liquid vehicle. |
| **Pure hazelnut paste** | **90g** | Provides the flavour and the bulk of the lipids. |
| **Cream (35% fat)** | **30g** | Minimal; just a touch of dairy fat. |
| **Skimmed milk powder** | **30g** | MSNF: proteins that retain the air. |
| **Sucrose** | **54g** | Balanced base sweetness. |
| **Dextrose** | **49g** | High PAC; prevents large ice crystals. |
| **Inulin** | **23g** | Clean solids and body. |
| **Fine salt** | **1.5g** | Wakes up the toasted notes of the nut. |
| **Guar gum** | **0.5g** | 1/4 teaspoon. |

---

## Protocol

1. **Dispersion (50 °C):** heat the milk and the cream. At 50 °C, add the well-mixed dry powders (sucrose, dextrose, milk powder, inulin, salt and guar). Whisk.
2. **Pasteurisation (85 °C):** raise to 85 °C for 1-2 minutes, stirring constantly.
3. **Shearing:** turn off the heat, add the hazelnut paste and run the immersion blender at full power for **2 minutes** to integrate the oil with the milk.
4. **Maturation:** cool (reverse bain-marie with ice) and mature in the fridge for a minimum of **12 hours**.
5. **Churning:** put the mix in the **freezer for 15 minutes**, give it 15 seconds of immersion blender to break the density, and into the machine for **15-20 minutes**.
